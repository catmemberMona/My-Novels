Runa
Overview
Appearance

One of the first things youll notice about Runa is her grey hair, an unusual hair color in the city and towns.
If seen under the sunlight, people will see glimps of blue shade shining on her hair.
Her pupuils are pure black, but it has a layer of shine that gives her a lively appearance full of attention.
Her skin is slightly on the paler side of those that live in the city, with a barely noticable grey undertone, lacking the usual red or yellow tones.
She stands at the same height as Casey at 5'8" with a slim built but compact muscles.
Usually, Runa would be wearing dark grey tights with a loose torquoise colored shirt.
A wide leather [belt] is tied around her waist knotted in the back with thin ropes.
She often uses the leather belt to hold objects and hidden weapons. She would always be seen wearing a green sliver bracelet that she doesn't allow others to touch.

Personality

The first impression people would have of her would be outgoing, proactive and slightly flirty, which is accompanied by a polite smile.
She is a great listener, but this develops from her habit of gathering information aka. info hunting.
In times of danger, she is brave, calm and speaks clearly which are aspects that aids her gift in strategies and leading.
Even though she comes up with great ideas, it contributes to her frustration and anxiety by her constant overthinking.
This leads to her not being able to fully trust others.
When relaxed, she shows her childish side which is accompanied by mischeifs and kind heart.
